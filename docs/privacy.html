Privacy Policy for BD News Reader

Last Updated: January 14, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to BD News Reader ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address, display name when you create an account
  • Profile Information: Optional profile picture, preferences
  • User Content: Saved articles, bookmarks, reading preferences

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App interactions, features used, reading history
  • Analytics Data: App performance, crash reports via Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for:

  • Providing and maintaining the app service
  • Personalizing your news feed based on preferences
  • Syncing your data across devices (if signed in)
  • Sending push notifications (with your permission)
  • Analyzing app usage to improve features
  • Detecting and preventing technical issues

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, cloud storage, analytics, crashlytics
  • Google AdMob: Displaying advertisements (free tier only)
  • RSS Feed Providers: Fetching news content from various sources

Each service has its own privacy policy governing data collection.

5. Data Storage and Security

  • User data is stored in Firebase Cloud Firestore (encrypted at rest)
  • Passwords are handled by Firebase Authentication (not stored by us)
  • Local data cached on device using encrypted storage
  • We use HTTPS/SSL for all network communications

6. Your Rights (GDPR/CCPA)

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data (Settings → Privacy & Data → Export Data)
  • Delete: Request deletion of your account (Settings → Privacy & Data → Delete Account)
  • Opt-Out: Disable analytics, personalized ads, notifications
  • Portability: Export your data in JSON format

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, contact us immediately.

8. Advertising

Free tier users see ads via Google AdMob. Premium subscribers have an ad-free experience. You can control ad personalization in your device settings or through our app settings.

9. Data Retention

  • Account data: Retained until account deletion
  • Analytics data: Retained for 14 months (Firebase default)
  • Cached content: Cleared when cache is cleared

10. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and stored on servers located outside your country. We use Firebase's infrastructure which complies with relevant data protection regulations.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. You'll be notified of significant changes via the app or email. Continued use constitutes acceptance of changes.

12. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or requests:

  • Email: privacy@bdnewsreader.com
  • In-App: Settings → Help & Support → Contact Us
