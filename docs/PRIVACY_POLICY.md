# Privacy Policy for BD News Reader

**Last Updated:** January 12, 2026

## Introduction

BD News Reader ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

- **Account Information:** Email address and name (if you sign in with Google)
- **Preferences:** Your theme preferences, language settings, and notification preferences
- **Favorites & History:** Articles you save and read

### Automatically Collected Information

- **Usage Data:** App interactions, features used, search queries
- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system, app version
- **Performance Data:** Crash reports and performance metrics
- **Analytics:** Anonymous usage statistics via Firebase Analytics

## How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

- Provide and improve our services
- Personalize your experience (theme, language, recommendations)
- Send push notifications (if enabled)
- Analyze app usage and performance
- Fix bugs and crashes
- Comply with legal obligations

## Data Storage and Security

- **Local Storage:** Favorites, history, and preferences are stored on your device
- **Cloud Sync:** If signed in, your data syncs via Firebase (encrypted in transit)
- **Security Measures:** We use industry-standard security practices including HTTPS and encrypted storage

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

- **Firebase (Google):** Authentication, Analytics, Crashlytics, Cloud Messaging
- **Google AdMob:** Advertising (if applicable)
- **News Sources:** We fetch news from external RSS feeds

Each service has its own privacy policy:

- Firebase: <https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy>
- Google Privacy Policy: <https://policies.google.com/privacy>

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access:** View your data
- **Delete:** Remove your account and data
- **Export:** Download your data
- **Opt-out:** Disable analytics and notifications

### How to Exercise Your Rights

- Open app → Settings → Privacy & Data
- Or email: <support@bdnewsreader.com>

## Data Retention

- **Account Data:** Retained while account is active
- **Analytics:** Anonymized after 14 months
- **Crash Reports:** Retained for 90 days
- **Local Data:** Deleted when you uninstall the app

## Children's Privacy

BD News Reader is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy. Changes will be posted in the app with an updated "Last Updated" date.

## Contact Us

For questions about this privacy policy:

- Email: <privacy@bdnewsreader.com>
- App: Settings → Help & Support

## GDPR Compliance (EU Users)

If you are in the European Union, you have additional rights under GDPR:

- Right to data portability
- Right to object to processing
- Right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

## CCPA Compliance (California Users)

California residents have the right to:

- Know what personal information is collected
- Know if personal information is sold or disclosed
- Opt-out of the sale of personal information
- Non-discrimination for exercising CCPA rights

We do not sell your personal information.
